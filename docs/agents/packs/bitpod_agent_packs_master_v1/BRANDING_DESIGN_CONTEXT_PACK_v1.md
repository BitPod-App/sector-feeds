---

## 1) Brand position (working theory)

BitPod is *Bitcoin-focused intelligence*, not “portfolio flex.”  
Target user: curious, risk-tolerant, often younger, anti-Wall-Street, high agency.

Key tension to balance:
- **Not TradingView** (too sterile / institutional)
- **Not Mario** (too cartoon / unserious)
- “More fun than numbers” but still **serious enough to trust**.

---

## 2) The designer’s job: restrain without killing the soul

The agent should:
- prevent CJ from going too cartoonish
- preserve playfulness (decks / antennas / broadcast motifs)
- keep the result sleek, elegant, and coherent

Think:
- **modern + minimal** with **one playful accent**, not a theme park.

---

## 3) Cultural fit constraints (Bitcoin-native, not crypto-bro)

Avoid:
- casino vibes
- neon-meme overload
- generic “stock app” aesthetics

Prefer:
- clarity, craft, subtle symbolism
- motifs that feel like tools: instruments, dials, dashboards, signal towers, studio gear

---

## 4) Sonic identity angle (designer background)

The Branding & Design agent “studied sound design.” Translate that into visual sensibilities:
- pacing (space/whitespace)
- rhythm (repetition/structure)
- texture (material choices: matte vs gloss)
- “mixing” (foreground vs background hierarchy)

---

## 5) Deliverables (Branding & Design agent)

Week 1–2 deliverables (even if agent not fully active yet):
1) **Moodboard (10–20 refs)** with notes: why each fits the tension.
2) **Style axis** (2x2): serious↔playful vs minimal↔expressive.
3) **Logo direction set** (3 directions):
   - A: minimal signal/broadcast
   - B: “deck” motif (audio/records)
   - C: hybrid (signal + deck)
4) **Typography recommendation** (1 primary, 1 secondary) + usage rules.
5) **Color palette** (5–8 swatches) with do/don’t.
6) **Icon set rules** (stroke, corner radius, sizing).
7) **Export spec** (file naming, sizes: app icon, social avatar, OG image).

---

## 6) Governance & gates

- No brand assets are “final” without CJ approval.
- Always provide 3 options, each with tradeoffs.
- Keep assets versioned and named. No loose PNG chaos.
